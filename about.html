I am a Nigerian-American creative born + raised in Los Angeles, now based in NYC. Currently, I’m a rising second-year student at Columbia University.

I’ve always been drawn to creativity, and my curiosity drives me to ask big questions and find creative solutions, whether I’m building a project that blends tech and fashion, or teaching web development to students. I’m also passionate about fostering diversity, equity, and inclusion as the seeds for belonging.

This is why I created a platform called Hair-itage—to celebrate the history and cultural significance of Black hair—because I believe our stories deserve to be seen and heard (stay tuned for development changes!).

My mission is to use my skills and passions to build a world where everyone feels seen, valued, and connected. I’m excited about exploring the intersection of fashion and technology.
